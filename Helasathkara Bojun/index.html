Hela Sathkaara Bojun

Helasathkara is a unique and eco-friendly restaurant that uses filter water to cook all of their dishes and use qulity sri lankan spices. By cooking with filtered water, Helasathkara is able to enhance the natural flavors of the ingredients while also promoting better health and wellbeing. From mouth-watering curries to fresh seafood dishes, each meal at Helasathkara is prepared with care and attention to detail, ensuring that every customer has a delicious and healthy dining experience.
